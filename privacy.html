Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Trends (the "Service") respects your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, authentication identifiers.

  • Post data: captions, workout logs, media files, timestamps, visibility settings.
  • Usage and diagnostics: error logs and performance events used for service stability.

2. How We Use Data

  • To operate account login, posting, feed, and profile features.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and abuse prevention.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce Terms of Service.

3. Advertising and Cookies

This Service may display ads via third-party partners (including Google AdSense). Cookies and similar identifiers may be used for ad delivery, frequency control, fraud prevention, and measurement.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with infrastructure and analytics providers strictly to run and secure the Service.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal, security, and backup purposes.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
